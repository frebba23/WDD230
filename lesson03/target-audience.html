TARGET-AUDIENCE

Who is the target audience?

The main target audience is business owners who are thinking about starting or opening a business here in Masvingo. We want to connect them with marketing tools that are specific for our location, events like the annual Masvingo Business Show, tourists coming to Great Zimbabwe, and networking events like dinners for chamber members.

Two personas

  1. A start-up business owner, checks out Masvingo as a new possible place to open a new traditional food outlet. She knows that the tourist resort area offers a lot of opportunities in the food industry as tourists will be eager to taste traditional dishes as well as local people who want to connect with their heriatge.
  2. An already established business looking for ways to expand their business through collectively working together with other businesses in and out of their industry. they want to look for other opportunities and also make their business of selling local area relics and artifacts known nationally, regionally and also globally. they want to see this ans an opportunity to bring their business out of obscurity.

Scenarios

  1. Persona #1 wants to see if networking activities are there. She believes that through networking her business will be propelled forward very fast.
  2. Persona #1 wants to know what other companies are a part of the Chamber of Commerce.
  3. Persona #2 wants to see how much membership costs for each tier and what each tier includes, specifically marketing. Are all community events open to the Chamber of Commerce to market at?
  4. Persona #2 wants to grow their business and also possibly join it with other businesses. they want to see if the chamber of commerce provides them with that opportunity.
  5. Persona #2 wants to be involved in events where he could promote his business. Does the Chamber of Commerce page list that for its members?
